About Me

I'm an AI engineer based in Erlangen, Germany, completing my Master's in AI at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität.

My background is in Mechanical Engineering, and that foundation directly shapes how I work. Leading a 50-person maintenance team, I was already doing the core loop: collecting data, spotting anomalies, making decisions, and communicating findings to leadership. My shift to AI was about automating and scaling that same loop — which is exactly what my thesis does.

I think in systems, not just models. Years of managing real hardware, overhaul cycles, and cross-department projects taught me what it takes for something to actually work outside a lab — deployment, constraints, and reliability matter as much as accuracy.

Right now I'm focused on agentic AI systems that reason, retrieve, and act. I'm looking for roles where I can build things that work in production, not just in notebooks.
